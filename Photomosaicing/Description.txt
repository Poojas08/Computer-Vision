The program will do the following:
• Load the three images;
• Use ORB to find the correspondences between image0 and image1;
• Print to the console the displacement between image0 and image1;
• Merge these two images to produce a first merged result;
• Use ORB to find the correspondences between the first merged image and image2;
• Print to the console the displacement between the first merged image and image2;
• Merge these two images to produce a final result;
